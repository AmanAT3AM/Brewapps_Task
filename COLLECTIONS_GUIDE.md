# Collections Feature Guide

## What Collections Can Do

Collections allow you to organize your favorite quotes into custom categories. Here's everything you can do:

### 1. ✅ Create Collections
- Tap the **+** button in the Collections screen
- Enter a collection name (e.g., "Morning Motivation", "Work Quotes", "Love & Relationships")
- Tap "Create"

### 2. ✅ View Your Collections
- See all your collections in the main Collections screen
- Each collection shows:
  - Collection name
  - Creation date
  - Trash icon to delete

### 3. ✅ Add Quotes to Collections
- Open any collection by tapping on it
- Tap the **+** button in the collection detail view
- Browse or search for quotes
- Tap any quote to add it to the collection

### 4. ✅ Remove Quotes from Collections
- Open a collection
- Tap the **X** button on any quote card to remove it

### 5. ✅ Delete Collections
- Tap the trash icon on any collection card
- The collection and all its quotes are removed

### 6. ✅ Search Quotes When Adding
- Use the search bar in "Add Quotes" screen
- Search by quote text or author name

## Example Collection Ideas

- **Morning Motivation** - Quotes to start your day
- **Work Quotes** - Professional inspiration
- **Love & Relationships** - Romantic quotes
- **Success** - Achievement and goal quotes
- **Wisdom** - Life lessons and insights
- **Funny Quotes** - Humor collection

## Tips

1. Collections sync across devices when logged in
2. You can add the same quote to multiple collections
3. Quotes in collections remain in the main feed
4. Deleting a collection doesn't delete the quotes - they stay in the main database
